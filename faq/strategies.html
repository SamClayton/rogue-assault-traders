General strategies you can persue.

 
Strategies are general gaming styles that can allow you to get an idea of how to play the game effectively. The best players generally use a combination of the strategies we list here.

Strategies come and go. However, the four we offer have stood the test of time, and prove to be worthwhile. Please realize that no strategy can effectively guide a player through ALL of the game, just give guidelines and ideas that can be helpful.

No matter the strategy, teams of effective players can always be very helpful.
